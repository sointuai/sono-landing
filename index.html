Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for auto repair shops, wash bays, and dealers adopting Sono.

How does Sono help a car dealership?

Sono acts as an AI service advisor for auto repair shops and dealerships. She answers calls you miss, speaks naturally about maintenance or collision work, captures license plates and symptoms, and either books the job or logs a callback with full context.

What do I need to launch Sono in my workshop?

You can launch Sono in under two days: we train Sono on your service menu, pricing, and workshop capacity; we can sync key details from your website or DMS so she speaks your language; and you forward unanswered calls or use conditional forwarding following our 2-minute guide.

Can my advisors still answer calls themselves?

Yes. Sono only answers when your advisors or receptionists cannot pick up. If someone is available, the call routes to them first. You control the forwarding rules and can pause Sono whenever needed.

Does Sono book repairs or just collect requests?

You decide the workflow. Most workshops begin with Sono collecting detailed maintenance or repair requests so advisors can confirm them later. We can also enable direct scheduling so Sono books bays, courtesy cars, and technician types in real time.

How are call minutes counted for service calls?

Only the time Sono spends speaking with your customer counts toward the included minutes. If the caller hangs up quickly or your advisor takes over, those seconds are excluded. Minutes accrue per second.
